‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Can I recover my account after deletion?

No. Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. You would need to create a new account.

What happens to my incident reports?

Public incident reports remain visible but are anonymized (shown as "Anonymous User"). This preserves community safety history while protecting your privacy.

Will emergency services still have my old alerts?

Yes. Emergency alerts sent to ERCE/ERFCE may be retained for legal and investigative purposes, but your identity is anonymized after account deletion.

Can I delete specific data without deleting my account?

Yes! You can:

  • Update your profile information in-app
  • Remove your profile photo
  • Disable location tracking (Settings ‚Üí Privacy)
  • Contact us to delete specific data: riskplaceangola@gmail.com

How long does deletion take?

  • In-app deletion: Immediate
  • Email request: Up to 30 days (typically 7 business days)

Will I be notified when deletion is complete?

Yes, you will receive a confirmation email at your registered email address.
